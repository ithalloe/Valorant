come meet the duelists!

Jett Jett is originally from Insa-dong, a neighborhood located in Seoul, the capital of South Korea. Interestingly, this information is confirmed by one of its sprays, whose Korean characters represent the name of the neighborhood. Before becoming part of the Valorant Protocol, the young woman, whose real name is Sunwoo Han, made her living as a chef. Jett only carries smoke grenades and the ultimate knives. Despite this, make no mistake: she can get in and out of situations efficiently and create advantage in favor of her team.
Reyna Zyanya Mondragón is a Radiant from Mexico. After being affected by a disastrous event involving machinery. she has acquired a strong dislike of technology and humanity, and has come to believe that she and her fellow Radiants are the true future for Earth. Before being drafted into the VALORANT Protocol, she set up a sanctuary to help people escape the Realm and heal, establishing a community there. little more.
Neon Raised in Manila, Philippines, Tala Nicole Dimaapi Valdez is a Radiant empowered by her bioelectricity who has bonded with Earth's radianite lattice. A young but former K-SEC veteran, she was recruited by Protocol VALORANT as part of their Project Omega, specifically to utilize her electrical abilities as they attempted to power an Alpha-Omega teleporter. Accepting a position there as one of six duelists present in the agent game, "Neon",
Raze Tayane Alves is an engineer from Salvador, Bahia, Brazil, with a strong enthusiasm for explosives and paints - often in combination. Holding strong objections to the Kingdom Corporation's operations in Bahia, Alves was involved in expelling the company from its home state and forcing it to abandon its sites there, as its toolmarks stained the lots as proof of its victories. Hired since then to work for the VALORANT Protocol as one of six Duelists, Agent "Raze"
Phoenix Jamie Adeyemi is a radiant with abilities associated with fire, he hails from the Peckham district of London, England. Growing up, Adeyemi attended a performing arts school in the area, but was later suspended. Recruited for the VALORANT Protocol as part of the six duelists present in the game, agent "Phoenix",
Yoru from Tokyo, Japan, Kiritani Ryo is a man dedicated to uncovering his past. Specifically interested in his ancestors and an ancient order of samurai, Kiritani's quest continues to lead him to the Kingdom's S22 container port. On his first visit, he broke into the facility and stole a mask from a samurai armor set that allowed him to see into another dimension. With that, he was recruited by the VALORANT Protocol to be part of the six duelists present in the agent game "Yoru".
